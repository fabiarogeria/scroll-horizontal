Grammy 2025

Beyoncé wins Album of the Year for Cowboy Carter

  • After six nominations without a win, Beyoncé finally took home the Album of the Year award at the 2025 GRAMMYs with Cowboy Carter.
  • The ceremony took place in Los Angeles, and she took the stage accompanied by her daughter Blue Ivy.
  • In her speech, Beyoncé said: “I feel fulfilled and very honored. It’s been many, many years.”
  • She dedicated the award to singer Linda Martell, a Black pioneer in country music, whose story inspired the album.
